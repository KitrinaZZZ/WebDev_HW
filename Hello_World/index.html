Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. Mix flour and sugar in a bowl
  3. Add chocolate chips and stir
  4. Place spoonfuls of dough on baking tray
  5. Bake for 10–12 minutes
