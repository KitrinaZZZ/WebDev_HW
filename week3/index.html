Breakfast

Soft Morning Yogurt Bowl (5 min)

Recipe

Time5 min
LevelEasy
Serves1
Vibecozy

Ingredients

  • Greek yogurt (or plant yogurt) — 1 cup
  • Banana — 1 (sliced)
  • Granola — 1/3 cup
  • Honey or maple syrup — 1 tsp
  • Optional: berries, cinnamon, nuts

Steps

  1. Spoon yogurt into a bowl.
  2. Add banana + granola on top.
  3. Drizzle honey/maple syrup.
  4. Finish with berries/cinnamon (optional).
Little tip ✿

Want it extra cute? Make a “smiley face” with banana + berries.

Lunch

Gentle Salmon Rice Bowl (15 min)

Recipe

Time15 min
LevelEasy
Serves1–2
Vibewarm

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice — 1 bowl
  • Salmon — 1 fillet (or tofu)
  • Avocado — 1/2
  • Cucumber — a few slices
  • Soy sauce + sesame oil — small splash
  • Optional: sesame seeds, scallion

Steps

  1. Pan-sear salmon 3–4 min each side (or air-fry).
  2. Add rice to bowl; top with salmon + veggies.
  3. Mix soy sauce + sesame oil as a quick sauce.
  4. Sprinkle sesame seeds. Done!

Note: tofu works great if you want vegetarian.

Flavor idea

Try adding a tiny squeeze of lemon—super fresh but still soft.

Dinner

Cloudy Tomato Egg Soup (12 min)

Recipe

Time12 min
LevelEasy
Serves2
Vibesoft

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes — 2 (chopped)
  • Eggs — 2
  • Water or stock — 3 cups
  • Salt — to taste
  • Optional: scallion, a tiny sugar

Steps

  1. Simmer tomatoes in water/stock 5–6 min.
  2. Beat eggs; slowly pour while stirring to form ribbons.
  3. Season with salt (tiny sugar optional).
  4. Top with scallion. Cozy dinner ready.
Little tip ✿

If you want it thicker, add a tiny cornstarch slurry.

Why this fits “cute low-sat”

Soft colors + round shapes + gentle shadows = cozy feeling.
